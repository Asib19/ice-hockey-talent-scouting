Additional Feature Descriptions: 

helmet_brand: The manufacturer of the player's protective helmet
tape_color: The color of the friction tape applied to the stick blade or handle
uses_tinted_visor: Boolean flag indicating if the player uses a tinted visor
skate_hollow_inches: The depth of the groove ground into the skate blade
stick_blade_curve_mm: The maximum distance of the blade's curve from a straight line
extra_sticks_packed: The quantity of spare sticks prepared by the player for a game or trip
brings_backup_stick: Boolean indicating if the player carries a backup stick to the bench
avg_shoot_speed: Average velocity of the puck shot by player
training_pct: Percentage of a life time a Player has spent in training
pass_drill_success_pct: Percentage of successful completions during passing exercises
video_review_completion_pct: Ratio of assigned game footage watched by the player
warmup_laps: Total number of laps skated during the warm-up
hydration_pct: Metric representing the player's fluid saturation levels
pregame_music_genre: The primary style of music consumed by the player before puck drop
locker_room_role: The player's social/leadership designation
left_skate_first: If the player ties their left skate before the right
morning_skate_attended: Boolean indicating if the player participated in the early-day practice.
bus_seat_number: The designated or preferred seating location on team transit
autographs_signed_week: Total volume of fan signatures provided over a seven-day window
sleep_hours_before_game: Total duration of rest recorded the night prior to a game
travel_fatigue_score: Calculated index of physiological impact from recent travel
recovery_balance_minutes: Time spent in active recovery (physio, cold plunge) vs. exertion
shot_power_score:A numerical value representing the force of a player's shot
